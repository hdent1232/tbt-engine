Getting started

  1. Debts — add every debt (or import from your credit report): balance, APR, minimum payment, due day.
  2. Bills — add all monthly expenses: rent, wifi, electric, water, gas, car payment, insurance…
  3. Settings — set how often you're paid and your essentials budget.
  4. Paycheck — every time you get paid, enter the amount. The app tells you exactly where each dollar goes.
  5. Spending — import bank statement CSVs to see where money leaks and what to cut.

I got paid — what do I do with it?

“Preview” shows the plan without saving. “Create plan” saves it, updates bill set-asides, applies payments to your debt balances and moves the emergency-fund contribution.
